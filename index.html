OakVyapar ERP - Account Deletion

OakVyapar ERP allows users to permanently delete their account directly from within the application.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open OakVyapar ERP.
  2. Go to Account.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion request.

What Happens After Deletion?

  • Your Firebase Authentication account will be permanently deleted.
  • Your registered email address, name and phone number associated with your account will be removed.
  • Your business data is stored locally on your own device and is not stored on OakVyapar servers.
  • If you wish to remove local business data, uninstall the app or clear the app's storage from your device settings.

Need Help?

If you are unable to access your account, you can request account deletion by emailing:

picograin.in@gmail.com

Please include your registered email address in your request.
